MALDIVES

December to March is the best time to visit Maldives, as this period marks the peak tourist season. The Republic of Maldives, a small nation located in the Arabian Sea of the Indian Ocean, home to more than 1000 reef fringed islands, the Maldives tour offers some of the best beaches, luxurious over-water villas, beautiful corals, and azure waters.

KERALA

Winter season between September to March is the best time to visit Kerala. This is the time when Kerala has a comfortable climate and pleasant weather with less humidity. If you wish to explore the enchanting lagoons, tranquil canals and spicy plantations of Kerala's amazing cities, winter is the perfect time for you.

SHIMLA

The best time to visit Shimla is during May to June and December to January when romance and beauty are baked into every inch of the soil here. Clear blue skies in summer, sprinkles of powdery snow in the winter, the spicy and earthy scent carried by pines, firs, cedars and oak trees.
